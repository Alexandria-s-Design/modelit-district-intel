## The Hook

> "Banning USD outperformed both Riverside County and the entire state in math growth for 2024-25 — +7.75% in 11th grade and +5.96% in 7th grade. Your district has proven that interventions work and students can improve. But only 15.3% overall are meeting math standards — 19 points below state average. ModelIt extends that success by adding the computational layer your students need. Students BUILD models of real systems — not just click through simulations. They use variables, equations, and graphs to solve problems they care about. And with 75% Hispanic students, ModelIt's fully bilingual interface means every student can build models in English or Spanish — making computational thinking accessible to all."

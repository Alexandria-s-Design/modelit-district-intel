## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

ModelIt should be positioned as an **NGSS-aligned computational modeling supplement** that enhances district's science and math instruction:

1. **NGSS gap filler**: NGSS computational modeling (SEP #2) is likely missing from current instruction. ModelIt provides this critical practice.
2. **Math intervention**: 15.3% math proficiency is crisis. ModelIt makes math meaningful through applied modeling contexts.
3. **Bilingual imperative**: 74.9% Hispanic — district NEEDS bilingual tools. ModelIt's EN/ES interface is a major differentiator.
4. **CTE enhancement**: For high school grades, ModelIt can enhance CTE pathways in biotechnology, health science, environmental science.
5. **Math growth positive**: District outperformed county and state in math growth — suggests interventions work and district is open to innovation.

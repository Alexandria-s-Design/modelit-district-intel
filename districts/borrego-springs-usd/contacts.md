## Key Relationships & Notes

- **Dr. Stevens is the single point of entry**: There is no curriculum director, no STEM coordinator, no assistant superintendent. The superintendent makes all instructional decisions.
- **Remote community dynamics**: Borrego Springs is isolated (~90 min from San Diego). Personal visits carry enormous weight because few vendors make the trip.
- **Board recently refreshed**: 3 of 5 trustees were elected/re-elected in Nov 2024. New board may be receptive to new initiatives.
- **Staff directory is not public**: The website has a staff directory page but it doesn't render staff details. A phone call or in-person visit is required to identify teachers.
- **Community Schools model**: The district is a Community Schools site, meaning they have strong family/community engagement infrastructure that could help with ModelIt adoption.

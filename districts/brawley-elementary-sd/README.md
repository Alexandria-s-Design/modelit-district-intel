## Why This District — 7 Reasons ModelIt Fits

1. **Science gap is severe and solvable** — Only 12.93% met/exceeded on CAST science (state avg ~29%); critically, **60% of students scored "Nearly Met"** — they are one tier away; the right tool can unlock this cohort
2. **Math collapse in middle grades** — Proficiency drops from 32% in Grade 3 to 12% in Grade 8; ModelIt's math-science integration directly targets the grade band where the crisis accelerates (Grades 5-8)
3. **95% Hispanic, ~39% English Learners** — ModelIt's bilingual (English/Spanish) interface is a direct equity differentiator; EL students score only **1.61% on science** — near zero; no bilingual science platform currently documented at BESD
4. **No confirmed science curriculum publisher** — White space opportunity; K-8 science curriculum is undocumented in all public records; ModelIt can be the first dedicated science tool to enter
5. **New Padilla-Pace Middle School (opened Aug 2024)** — First new school in 64 years; tech-ready campus (ceiling projectors, dedicated science classrooms); **no legacy curriculum lock-in** — perfect pilot site
6. **Superintendent has curriculum/instruction + technology DNA** — Luis Panduro was Director of Instructional Technology AND Asst. Supt. of Curriculum and Instruction; speaks both languages and understands EdTech investment value
7. **GEAR UP / Project R.I.S.E. active** — Active $12.8M federal STEM enrichment grant (ICOE + SDSU-IV + IVC); STEM enrichment already a funded priority for middle school students

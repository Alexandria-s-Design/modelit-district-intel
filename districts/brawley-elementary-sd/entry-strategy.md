## Decision Process

1. **Staff evaluation** — Ramirez + Lizarraga review tool, see demo, assess NGSS/device compatibility
2. **Principal buy-in** — Vildosola at Padilla-Pace MS agrees to pilot; Casey at Barbara Worth as backup
3. **Superintendent approval** — Panduro sign-off (likely easy given his background; keep him warm throughout)
4. **Budget identification** — Dickerson (Fiscal) identifies Title IV-A or LCFF Supplemental funds
5. **LCAP alignment** — Mason or Larios ensure action item fits 2026-27 LCAP or current year carryover
6. **Purchase order / contract** — Dickerson processes; under threshold = no board vote needed
7. **Implementation** — PD training at Padilla-Pace; teacher onboarding; student rollout

**Fast track**: A $5K–$8K single-school pilot (Padilla-Pace MS, Grade 6) can likely be approved by Panduro + Dickerson without full board vote. Target this pathway first.

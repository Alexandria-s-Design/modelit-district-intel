### LCFF Supplemental & Concentration Grants

BESD's high unduplicated pupil percentage (EL + SED students) generates significant supplemental and concentration funding. Key figures:
- English Learners: ~39.3% (1,533 of 3,883 students)
- Economically Disadvantaged: ~84.6%
- These rates trigger **concentration funding** (ED > 55% threshold) — this is substantial discretionary funding

**Concentration grant estimate**: At ~84.6% ED, BESD is well above the 55% threshold for concentration grants. This is the largest discretionary funding pool for equity-targeted programs.

### NGSS Adoption Status

California adopted NGSS in 2013 and requires full NGSS implementation. BESD's LCAP lists "Implementing Common Core/NGSS standards successfully" as a goal, but no NGSS-aligned publisher or curriculum resource is publicly documented. This means either:
1. Teachers are building curriculum independently from state resources (Smarter Balanced Digital Library, CDE frameworks)
2. An older, pre-NGSS curriculum is still in use (FOSS, Scott Foresman Science, etc.)
3. A newer adoption is in progress but not publicly announced

Given the 12.93% CAST score (16 points below state average), the current science approach is not producing results.

## What This Means for ModelIt — Positioning Strategy

### The Core Problem ModelIt Solves

The CAST assesses **Science and Engineering Practices** (SEPs), not just content knowledge. The top two SEPs tested:
- **SEP-2: Developing and Using Models** — students must represent systems, relationships, and mechanisms
- **SEP-5: Using Mathematics and Computational Thinking** — students must use quantitative reasoning in science

BESD students at 12.93% proficiency are not failing because they lack content — the "Nearly Met" data shows they have baseline knowledge. They are failing at the **practices**. ModelIt is the only K-12 platform specifically built for SEP-2 (building systems models) and SEP-5 (embedding math in science). This is an exact match.

### ModelIt Complement Map — Current Tools vs. ModelIt

| Current Tool | What It Does | What ModelIt Adds |
|-------------|-------------|------------------|
| McGraw-Hill Connected (Math) | Procedural math practice | Applies math skills in science context — makes abstract math meaningful |
| Achieve3000 (Literacy) | Differentiated reading at level | Science vocabulary and concept reading in bilingual context |
| Renaissance STAR | Identifies reading/math gaps | Does not address science SEP gaps — ModelIt fills this |
| Holt McDougal (ELA/Science) | Content delivery textbook | ModelIt adds interactive modeling on top of static text |
| ASES / Smart Start / Camp Adventure | Enrichment and engagement | ModelIt delivers structured SEP-2/5 practice with measurable outcomes |
| CAST (assessment only) | Measures SEP performance | ModelIt is the instructional tool to close the SEP gap |

**Key message**: ModelIt does NOT replace any current tool. It is an SEP-2/5 practice layer that sits on top of existing science content.

## Local Environmental Science Hooks (ModelIt Content)

The Imperial Valley setting provides exceptional, locally relevant science contexts for ModelIt models:

| Topic | Science Standards | ModelIt Model Type |
|-------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Salton Sea restoration | Life science (ecosystems), Earth science (hydrology) | Ecosystem model with species interactions |
| Colorado River water allocation | Earth science (water systems), Math (rates, ratios) | Water flow + population demand model |
| Lithium Valley / geothermal energy | Physical science (energy), Environmental science | Energy extraction and environmental impact model |
| Agricultural ecosystem (Imperial Valley farms) | Life science (ecosystems), Chemistry (soils) | Farm ecosystem and nutrient cycle model |
| Desert climate (extreme heat, drought) | Earth science (climate), Life science (adaptation) | Climate system model |

These local contexts are emotionally resonant for 95% Hispanic students from farming and working-class families. Using them in a ModelIt demo is a powerful differentiation from generic curriculum materials.

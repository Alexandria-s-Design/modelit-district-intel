## Why This District (7 Reasons ModelIt Fits)

1. **Greenfield science curriculum** — No confirmed science curriculum vendor identified; district is actively building STEM infrastructure with Measure Q bond ($47M). ModelIt enters with zero incumbent conflict.

2. **60% "Nearly Met" in science** — The largest student cohort scored one tier below proficiency at every grade (5, 8, 11). Computational modeling is exactly the intervention needed to close this gap.

3. **Bilingual border community** — 99% Hispanic enrollment, with many families crossing from Mexicali. ModelIt is bilingual (English/Spanish) — unique among modeling platforms and essential for this district.

4. **17.23% math proficiency** — ModelIt addresses NGSS SEP-5 (Using Mathematics and Computational Thinking), making it a dual-impact tool for both math and science goal-setting.

5. **Measure Q STEM buildings** — Two new STEM buildings at Calexico High (robotics labs, wet labs) completing 2025. District is actively investing in STEM infrastructure — right moment for modeling software.

6. **LCAP equity focus** — High concentration of unduplicated pupils (SED + EL) = large Supplemental/Concentration grant under LCFF. These funds MUST be spent on services for unduplicated students — perfect funding pathway.

7. **SPARKs after-school STEM** — Active ASES-funded after-school program with Lego Education and STEM enrichment already running. ModelIt can expand this footprint via teacher PD + after-school pilot.

## Purchasing & Warrants — Extraction Template

When reviewing board consent calendars and warrant registers, flag the following categories for ModelIt intelligence:

| Category | What to Look For | Why It Matters |
|----------|-----------------|----------------|
| Curriculum Publisher Payments | Amplify, FOSS, National Geographic, Discovery Ed, Savvas/Pearson payments | Reveals current science curriculum vendor and contract size |
| EdTech Platform Costs | Google, Canvas, iReady, Renaissance, BrightBytes, Nearpod, PD providers | Maps the current edtech stack; identifies integration points |
| Technology Hardware | Chromebook, iPad, projector, smart display purchases | Confirms 1:1 device rollout progress |
| Assessment Platform | NWEA, Illuminate, Benchmark, FastBridge, iReady | Assessment vendors used; may create conflict or complement |
| STEM Equipment | Lab equipment, robotics kits, Lego Education, science consumables | Confirms STEM investment level; Measure Q spending |
| PD Vendor Payments | Professional development providers, curriculum coaches | Identifies PD spend available for ModelIt teacher training |
| After-School Contracts | Afterschool Unlimited, community orgs, tutoring | SPARKs budget size and partner details |

**Known Vendor Contracts (from research)**:
- Afterschool Unlimited: **$1.2M** — approved Aug. 2025 board meeting (SPARKs program)
- STEM buildings construction: **$65.4M total** (Measure Q bond proceeds; multiple contractor POs) — Calexico High

**Warrant Register Access**: Available via Simbli portal (https://simbli.eboardsolutions.com/SB_Meetings/SB_MeetingListing.aspx?S=36030714). Look for Consent Calendar items labeled "Ratification of Warrants" or "Purchase Orders" — these appear at every regular meeting.

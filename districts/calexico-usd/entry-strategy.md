## Why Calexico USD Is a Priority Target

1. **Critical science gap**: 13% CAST proficiency; 60% of students "Nearly Met" — actionable cohort
2. **No confirmed science curriculum** — greenfield vendor opportunity
3. **New STEM buildings** completing 2025 at Calexico High — "new space needs new tools" timing
4. **99% Hispanic enrollment + 8.8% EL** — border community with deep bilingual roots; ModelIt's English/Spanish interface is a direct differentiator vs. incumbents and resonates with Superintendent Jimenez (fluent Spanish speaker, son of migrant farmworkers)
5. **$19,484/student** + $185M total budget — financial capacity exists despite rural, low-income demographics
6. **LCFF Supplemental/Concentration grant** — 56.8% SED generates significant supplemental/concentration funds that MUST be spent on services for unduplicated pupils; ModelIt qualifies as direct academic intervention
7. **Title IV-A** explicitly allows EdTech purchases — district qualifies
8. **Technology-committed superintendent** — Jimenez: "committed to being a driving force in the use of technology in the classroom"
9. **Active board transition** (2 new members Jan. 2025) — fresh ears on new vendor conversations

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|---------|
| "We don't have budget" | "Title IV-A and LCFF concentration funds specifically target academic enrichment for unduplicated pupils (EL and SED). Your 56.8% SED rate generates significant supplemental and concentration dollars that must be spent on exactly these services — ModelIt qualifies." |
| "We already have a science curriculum" | "Can you tell me which program? [If TBD/weak:] Our data shows CAST proficiency at 13% — if your current program isn't moving students, ModelIt works alongside it as an intervention and enrichment layer, not a replacement." |
| "We're focused on ELA and math" | "We understand — they're tested more often. But CAST is tested 3x (grades 5, 8, 11) and California will be increasing science accountability. Getting ahead of science now positions your students for the graduation requirements ahead." |
| "The board needs to approve it" | "Absolutely. A pilot costs nothing and gives you the data to take to the board with confidence. Let's start small at one school." |
| "We're not ready" | "Your new STEM buildings are completing this year. When they open, teachers will need digital tools to fill them with learning. Let's have a conversation before your opening date." |

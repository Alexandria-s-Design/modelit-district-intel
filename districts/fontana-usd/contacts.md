## Key Relationships & Notes

- **No dedicated Math/Science Director exists**: Unlike some large districts, FUSD does not have a Math Director or Science Director. These functions fall under the STEAM Coordinator (Dolly Bergen) and the Teaching & Learning exec directors. This makes Bergen the most critical contact.
- **Teaching & Learning leadership is in transition**: The Assoc. Supt. position is interim (Hollister). Watch for a permanent hire -- this will be the new curriculum authority.
- **Danielle Holley is a potential board champion**: MA in Curriculum & Instruction, former FUSD teacher, Fontana Chamber Exec Dir. She understands both the educational and business case for STEM tools.
- **Adam Perez focuses on career pathways**: His stated priority on career pathways and mental health makes him a secondary board ally for CTE-focused ModelIt positioning.
- **Procurement goes through OpenGov**: FUSD uses OpenGov eProcurement for all purchasing. For purchases above threshold, this is the formal channel.
- **Dr. Alvarez bridge to both districts**: He connects Fontana (where he built his career) and Rialto (where he now leads). A single relationship opens two large district opportunities.
- **3 board seats up in Nov 2026**: Serna (Area 1), Perez (Area 2), and Sandoval (Area 5) all face re-election. New members could shift priorities.

## The Hook

> "Fontana Unified has world-class STEM infrastructure -- the Innovation Empire Makerspace, the world's first Bio Animakerspace, an aviation lab with flight simulators, a welding shop with AWS certification. Your students compete internationally in World Robot Olympiad. You've invested $408 million in Measure I to modernize STEAM labs and expand CTE. But 77% of your students still aren't meeting math standards. 50% score 'Not Met.' And at middle school -- where math proficiency drops from 35% in third grade to just 21% by eighth grade -- students are losing mathematical confidence before they ever reach your incredible high school programs. ModelIt bridges that gap. It's a bilingual computational modeling platform where students BUILD mathematical models of real systems -- the kind of systems thinking that makes makerspaces, robotics, and CTE pathways more powerful. Your students have the tools to MAKE things and the tests to MEASURE knowledge. What they're missing is the tool to MODEL systems -- to think computationally about the world before they build in it. And with 86% Hispanic students and 25% English Learners, ModelIt's full bilingual EN/ES interface means every student can access computational thinking in their language."

## Decision Process

Large unified district with formal procurement processes:

1. **STEAM Coordinator evaluates** → Dolly Bergen assesses ModelIt as STEM/science/math tool
2. **CTE Director assesses CTE fit** → Kim Lawe confirms pathway alignment (Engineering, Biotech, Mechatronics, etc.)
3. **Assoc. Supt. Teaching & Learning recommends** → Hollister (interim) or successor approves for pilot
4. **CBO confirms funding** → Leslie Barnes identifies budget line (LCFF, Title I, CTE, Measure I)
5. **Purchasing processes** → OpenGov eProcurement for formal bids if above threshold
6. **Superintendent approves** → Inbody signs off on tool and budget
7. **Board approval** → Required if purchase exceeds district threshold (likely $25-50K+ for large district)
8. **Implementation** → MS math/science + HS CTE deployment

**RFP likely required** for district-wide adoption given district size and budget. Pilot-scale purchases may be approved at superintendent level without full RFP. Position pilot as proof-of-concept to inform future RFP.

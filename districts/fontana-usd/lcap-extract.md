- Position as evidence-based math intervention under LCFF, MTSS, and NGSS frameworks

## Measure I Bond - Special Analysis

The $408M Measure I bond passed November 2024 with 66.42% approval. This is FUSD's first bond in nearly 20 years.

| Bond Priority | Relevance |
|--------------|-----------|
| CTE expansion: robotics, coding, medical, construction labs | *** HIGH - ModelIt is computational software for these labs |
| STEAM classroom modernization | *** HIGH - ModelIt is the instructional tool for modernized STEAM labs |
| Wireless internet infrastructure upgrades | ** MEDIUM - Enables cloud-based ModelIt deployment |
| Safety and accessibility improvements | * LOW |
| Roof replacements, HVAC upgrades | * LOW |
| Portable classroom replacement | * LOW |

**Key Insight**: Measure I explicitly funds CTE expansion (robotics, coding labs) and STEAM classroom modernization. ModelIt is the software that activates these hardware investments. Bond language and ModelIt positioning are perfectly aligned.

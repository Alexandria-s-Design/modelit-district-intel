## Key Talking Points

1. **"77% of your students aren't meeting math standards"**: "That's not a gap -- it's a crisis. And it's happening despite having some of the best STEM infrastructure in the Inland Empire. The Innovation Empire, the Bio Animakerspace, WRO robotics -- these are world-class. What's missing is the computational thinking platform that turns hands-on making into mathematical reasoning."

2. **"The middle school math cliff"**: "Math proficiency drops from 35% in third grade to just 21% by eighth grade. By the time students reach your incredible high school CTE programs, most have already lost confidence in math. ModelIt intervenes at middle school with engaging, visual computational models that rebuild math reasoning before high school."

3. **"$408M in Measure I needs software to match"**: "Measure I is upgrading STEAM labs and expanding CTE facilities across the district. But hardware without software is an empty room. ModelIt is the computational modeling platform that makes those $408 million in facilities investments more powerful."

4. **"Model it before you make it"**: "Your students have 3D printers, laser cutters, CNC machines, and flight simulators. ModelIt adds the simulation layer -- students model the system computationally before they fabricate physically. It's how engineering, biotech, and manufacturing actually work in industry."

5. **"Bilingual by design for YOUR students"**: "86.5% of your students are Hispanic. 25% are English Learners. Only 5% of your ELs meet math standards. ModelIt is fully bilingual -- English and Spanish -- which means every student can access computational thinking in their language. Discovery Education doesn't offer that."

6. **"Discovery Education + ModelIt"**: "You've had a great partnership with Discovery Education since 2015. STEM Connect provides content. Math Techbook provides practice. ModelIt provides something neither does -- students BUILD their own computational models. It's the creation layer on top of the content layer."

7. **"Oak Grove Prep Academy founding tool"**: "A brand new 7-12 STEM academy opening August 2026 with ICT, Health Science, and Arts/Media pathways. ModelIt can be part of the founding curriculum -- computational modeling embedded from day one."

8. **"Dr. Alvarez knows this district"**: "Dr. Alejandro Alvarez spent his entire early career at Fontana -- Tokay Elementary through Associate Superintendent. He's now leading Rialto next door. We're working with both districts because the Inland Empire needs computational STEM tools, and the math proficiency data makes the case."

## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

ModelIt should be positioned as a **computational modeling platform** that bridges FUSD's existing STEM infrastructure (robotics, makerspaces, bio-animatronics) with the academic math/science proficiency crisis:

1. **The missing middle layer**: FUSD has world-class STEM hardware (Innovation Empire, Bio Animakerspace, WRO robotics) but 77% of students can't meet math standards. ModelIt provides the computational thinking layer that connects hands-on making to mathematical reasoning.

2. **Discovery Education complement**: Discovery Ed provides digital content (STEM Connect) and math curriculum (Math Techbook). ModelIt adds model-BUILDING -- students create, not consume. Different cognitive engagement. Complementary, not competing.

3. **Middle school crisis intervention**: Math proficiency drops from 35% (Grade 3) to 21% (Grade 7-8). ModelIt at the middle school level uses engaging, visual computational models to rebuild math confidence and reasoning before high school.

4. **CTE pathway integration**: 22 CTE pathways, many STEM-aligned (Engineering, Biotech, Mechatronics, Health Science, Fire Tech). ModelIt adds computational modeling to existing hands-on CTE coursework.

5. **Bilingual advantage**: 86.5% Hispanic, 25% EL. ModelIt's bilingual EN/ES interface is a critical differentiator that Discovery Education and other STEM platforms don't offer.

6. **Oak Grove Prep Academy launch**: New STEM academy opening Aug 2026 with ICT, Health Science, and Arts/Media pathways. ModelIt can be part of the founding curriculum.

### Revenue Breakdown

| Source | % | Amount (2021-22) |
|--------|---|--------|
| State (LCFF base + supplemental + categorical) | 75% | $28,800,000 |
| Local (property taxes, fees) | 15% | $5,800,000 |
| Federal (Title I/II/IV, IDEA, ESSER) | 10% | $3,900,000 |

**Key insight**: State funding dominates at 75%, reflecting a low local property tax base. The 85.3% Hispanic + 23.7% EL + 61.3% ED populations generate significant LCFF Supplemental/Concentration funding. Per-pupil spending of $13,433 is below state median ($18,399), indicating a budget-constrained district.

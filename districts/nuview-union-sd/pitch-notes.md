## Key Talking Points

1. **"Your STEM Lab needs a computational layer"**: "You built a fantastic STEM Lab at Mountain Shadows — 120 students a day doing hands-on agriculture, homes, and oceanography. ModelIt adds the computational modeling that NGSS demands. Students go from observing to building models — that's the difference between doing science and thinking like a scientist."

2. **"Pre-NGSS science is holding you back"**: "Harcourt Science was the standard a decade ago, but NGSS has fundamentally changed what good science instruction looks like. ModelIt brings Practice #2 — Developing and Using Models — into your classrooms right now, without waiting for a full curriculum adoption."

3. **"85% Hispanic means bilingual isn't optional"**: "With 85.3% Hispanic students and 23.7% English Learners, your district needs instructional tools that work in both languages. ModelIt is fully bilingual — every button, label, and instruction in English and Spanish. Students can build models in the language they think in."

4. **"22% math proficiency needs a different approach"**: "Nearly 4 out of 5 students aren't meeting math standards. ModelIt makes math meaningful — students use variables, ratios, and graphs to build models of real systems. It's applied mathematics, not drill worksheets."

5. **"Solar savings → STEM Lab → ModelIt"**: "You showed how creative funding works: redirect energy savings to build a STEM Lab. ModelIt is the software that makes that investment even more productive. $5-8K for a tool that upgrades every STEM Lab session."

6. **"18% science proficiency is the red flag"**: "Science is your lowest subject — 18% proficiency. NGSS demands three-dimensional science instruction with modeling practices. ModelIt directly addresses this gap."

7. **"Perfect for grades 5-8"**: "ModelIt's sweet spot is exactly your district's grade span. Grades 5-8 students are developmentally ready for computational modeling — and it's where the math gap is most critical."

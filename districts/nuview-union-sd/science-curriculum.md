## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

ModelIt should be positioned as an **NGSS-aligned computational modeling supplement** that upgrades the district's pre-NGSS science instruction and extends the STEM Lab's capabilities:

1. **NGSS gap filler**: Harcourt Science is pre-NGSS. ModelIt provides NGSS SEP #2 (Developing and Using Models) that the current curriculum doesn't address. This is the MOST compelling argument.
2. **STEM Lab extension**: The Mountain Shadows STEM Lab already does hands-on learning. ModelIt adds the computational modeling layer — students go from hands-on to computational to mathematical.
3. **Agriculture module connection**: The STEM Lab includes sustainable agriculture modules. ModelIt lets students BUILD computational models of agricultural systems — extending the module into math reasoning.
4. **Oceanography connection**: STEM Lab includes oceanography modules. ModelIt can model ocean ecosystems, currents, temperature, and marine populations.
5. **Bilingual imperative**: 85.3% Hispanic + 23.7% EL — the district NEEDS bilingual tools. ModelIt's EN/ES interface is a major differentiator.

## Why ModelIt Fits This District (7 Reasons)

1. **Science scores are low and untapped** — Only 26.45% met/exceeded CAST standards. No confirmed NGSS science publisher in place = open white space for adoption.

2. **Math proficiency crashes in middle school** — From 43% in Grade 3 to just 22% by Grade 8. ModelIt's computational thinking approach addresses the exact skills that collapse in grades 6–8.

3. **62% Hispanic, 14.5% EL students** — ModelIt is bilingual (English/Spanish), directly serving the district's largest student group and EL population that scores only 7–9% on science and math.

4. **Scale matters** — 20,000 students across 26 campuses. A district-wide license is a $60–100K+ deal. Five junior high schools are natural pilot targets.

5. **NSF SBIR credibility** — Dr. Katie Russell's equity focus aligns with NSF-funded, research-backed tools. This is not a startup pitch — it's a peer-reviewed program.

6. **Active STEM/STEAM push** — The district promotes STEM/STEAM enhancement and participates in GATE with science enrichment. DonorsChoose shows 3,800+ science/math projects funded = demand exists at the teacher level.

7. **LCAP equity funding** — With 62% Hispanic enrollment and 14.5% EL population, LCFF Supplemental and Concentration grant funds are substantial and can fund ModelIt outside the general fund.

## Meeting Schedule

- **Regular Meetings:** 2nd Tuesday of each month at 6:00 PM
- **Additional Meetings (May–June):** Also 4th Tuesday at 6:00 PM

- **Agenda System:** Simbli eBoard (since July 2022) — requires portal login
- **Historical Agendas (pre-July 2022):** AgendaOnline — [agendaonline.net/public/Agency.aspx?PublicAgencyID=58](https://agendaonline.net/public/Agency.aspx?PublicAgencyID=58)

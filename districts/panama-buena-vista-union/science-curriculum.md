## What This Means for ModelIt

### Science Positioning Strategy

1. **Open curriculum = open door.** No confirmed incumbent science publisher means no loyalty barrier. ModelIt enters as the first real NGSS-aligned computational modeling tool the district adopts.

2. **NGSS SEP2 and SEP5 gap.** Whatever science program they use, it almost certainly lacks student-led computational modeling. ModelIt fills that gap without requiring curriculum replacement — it complements any existing core science text.

3. **Grade 8 CAST is the exam that counts.** With 21.67% math proficiency at Grade 8 and 26.45% overall CAST, the five junior high schools (Grades 7–8) are the most urgent intervention sites. A JHS pilot should lead.

4. **EL science at 7.48% is a crisis.** ModelIt is bilingual (English/Spanish). For a district with 14.5% EL students scoring 7.48% on CAST, a bilingual science modeling tool isn't a feature — it's an equity imperative.

5. **Math-science connection.** ModelIt's computational thinking approach addresses the math decline visible across grades 3–8. Teachers and administrators will respond to a tool that builds math reasoning through science context.

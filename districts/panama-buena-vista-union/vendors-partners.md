## Vendor Landscape Analysis

### Where ModelIt Fits
ModelIt enters as the **first computational science modeling platform** in a district that has:
- Established HMH/McGraw Hill dominance in math and ELA (not science)
- Canvas LMS already deployed — ModelIt can integrate or run alongside
- ClassLink SSO already deployed — ModelIt can be added to ClassLink app library
- No confirmed NGSS science curriculum incumbent — open market
- Strong Google Workspace infrastructure — compatible environment

### Warm Intro Paths
| Connection | Path |
|-----------|------|
| McGraw Hill rep | Ask if McGraw Hill science programs are in play; if not, ModelIt is complementary to their math/ELA tools |
| Canvas integration | "Works inside Canvas" removes IT objection |
| ClassLink | Can demo SSO integration easily |
| Grimm Foundation | Environmental science partnership = sustainability/agriculture angle for ModelIt ecology models |
| DonorsChoose | Teachers already fund their own science materials — shows demand that admin hasn't yet met |

### Competitive Threats
| Competitor | Risk Level | Mitigation |
|-----------|------------|-----------|
| Amplify Science | Medium | "Amplify replaces; ModelIt complements" — position as addition not replacement |
| Discovery Education Science | Low–Medium | "ModelIt adds the student-led modeling that Discovery lacks" |
| STEMscopes | Low | Primarily Texas-focused; less CA presence |
| OpenSciEd (free) | Medium | "Free doesn't include PD, bilingual support, or computational modeling layer" |
| iReady (Curriculum Associates) | Low | Math/ELA only; no science modeling |

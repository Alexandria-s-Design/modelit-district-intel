## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

ModelIt should be positioned as a **standards-aligned computational modeling supplement** that enhances district's K-6 science and math instruction:

1. **NGSS gap filler**: NGSS computational modeling (SEP #2) is likely missing from current instruction. ModelIt provides this critical practice.
2. **Extension of hands-on**: District values physical education and activity. ModelIt extends from physical to computational.
3. **Bilingual imperative**: 100% minority enrollment — district values diverse tools. ModelIt's EN/ES interface supports equity.
4. **Math intervention connection**: Math proficiency data TBD but likely needs intervention given rural demographics.
5. **Elementary focus**: Grades 5-6 are natural entry point for ModelIt pilot.

## Contact Tiers

> **K-12 District Note**: Rim of the World is a 7-school, K-12 unified district serving ~2,877 students across the San Bernardino Mountains (Lake Arrowhead, Running Springs, Crestline). The tight-knit mountain community means relationships are deeply interconnected.

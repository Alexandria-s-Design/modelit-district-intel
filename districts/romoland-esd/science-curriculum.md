## What This Means for ModelIt

### Positioning Strategy

ModelIt should be positioned as an **NGSS-aligned computational modeling supplement** that enhances district's hands-on science instruction:

1. **NGSS gap filler**: Hands-on science is emphasized but NGSS computational modeling (SEP #2) is likely missing. ModelIt provides this critical practice.
2. **Extension of hands-on**: District values hands-on learning (manipulatives, labs). ModelIt extends this from physical to computational.
3. **Bilingual imperative**: 72.2% Hispanic + 29.7% EL — district NEEDS bilingual tools. ModelIt's EN/ES interface is a major differentiator.
4. **Math intervention connection**: 15.3% math proficiency is crisis. ModelIt makes math meaningful through applied modeling contexts.
5. **Middle school focus**: Grades 7-8 at Romoland Middle are natural entry point for ModelIt pilot.

### How ModelIt Complements Current Tools

| Current Tool/Program | What It Does | What ModelIt Adds |
|---------------------|-------------|-------------------|
| Hands-On Science (K-8) | Laboratory-based, physical science | NGSS-aligned computational modeling layer |
| Manipulative Mathematics | Concrete math tools | Digital modeling that bridges concrete → abstract |
| GATE program | Advanced learner support | Computational modeling challenges gifted students |
